About Me

Data that speaks to the business

I'm a Business Intelligence Developer with a passion for bridging the gap between raw data and meaningful business outcomes. My work spans the full data pipeline — from designing scalable SQL data models to crafting intuitive dashboards that empower stakeholders at every level.

I specialise in Tableau and Power BI, building reports that are not just visually clean, but logically sound and performance-optimised. I pair that with strong expertise in SQL and Databricks to handle complex transformations at scale.

Whether it's a C-suite KPI tracker or a granular operational report, I care deeply about clarity, accuracy, and adoption — dashboards only add value when people actually use them.
